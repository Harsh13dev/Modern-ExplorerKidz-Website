Play Group

Ages 2-3 Years

A nurturing introduction to preschool, focusing on socialization, sensory play, and motor skills development through fun activities like music, storytelling, and crafts.

  • Safe, colorful environment
  • Group play and sharing skills
  • Basic shapes and colors
Learn More

Nursery

Ages 3-4 Years

Building on early skills with structured play, this program introduces basic literacy, numeracy, and creativity through art, puzzles, and group activities.

  • Alphabet and number recognition
  • Fine motor skill development
  • Creative expression through art
Learn More

Junior Kindergarden (JR.KG)

Ages 4-5 Years

A dynamic program focusing on early academics, social skills, and creativity, preparing children for formal schooling with activities like reading, writing, and role-play.

  • Early reading and writing skills
  • Problem-solving activities
  • Teamwork and leadership
Learn More

Senior Kindergarden (SR.KG)

Ages 5-6 Years

Designed to prepare children for primary school, this program emphasizes advanced literacy, math, and critical thinking through interactive projects and group learning.

  • Advanced phonics and math skills
  • Science and nature exploration
  • Confidence-building activities
